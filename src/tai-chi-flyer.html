About the Program

Meditation in Motion — With a Strong Foundation

Tai Chi is an ancient Chinese healing art often described as "meditation in motion." This 5-week online program is designed to help you build a strong foundation for your Tai Chi practice through gentle warm-ups, standing meditation (Zhan Zhuang), foundational Tai Chi principles, and a beginner-friendly Tai Chi form to put those principles into practice.

Whether you're new to Tai Chi or have learned forms before and want a deeper understanding, this program will help you move with greater ease, confidence, and flow. Modifications will be provided.
